Start Building?

There are a few important things to consider when getting involved in the mechanical keyboard community. The hardest step is figuring out exactly what you want with your first custom mechanical keyboard. You'll need to figure out: What features you prefer? When you want to build your keyboard? Building a keyboard is very time-consuming, so think about it before you decide.
